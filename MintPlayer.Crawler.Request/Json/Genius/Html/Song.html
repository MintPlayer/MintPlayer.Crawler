About “Get Lucky”

Daft Punk get together with Pharrell Williams and Nile Rodgers for the first single from their 2013 Grammy Award winning LP, Random Access Memories.

  • Did Pharrell Williams have anything to do with the production of this song?

    When Pharrell is featured on a song, it is because of production. This is the exemption, however. Daft Punk does recognize The Neptunes as an inspiration, but Pharrell was used in “Get Lucky” and “Lose Yourself to Dance” as a voice of one of the greatest producers of modern times that could provide real soul vocals, rather than production, to Daft Punk’s throwback project.
